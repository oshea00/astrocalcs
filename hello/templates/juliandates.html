Julian Dates

Astronomers have chosen the fundamental epoch as Greenwich mean noon of January 1, 4713 BCE (-4712 ). That is midday on the Greenwich meridian. The number of days elapsed since then are referred to as the Julian date. Note that BCE years are entered as -(year BCE - 1).
